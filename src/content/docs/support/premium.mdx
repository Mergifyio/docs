Mergify's 24/7 Premium Support gives your team a direct escalation path to our
on-call engineers — around the clock, every day of the year. This service is
exclusively for critical incidents that block your ability to merge.

For general support questions, configuration help, or feature requests, see the
[standard support page](/support).

## How to Escalate

Email our dedicated escalation address:

<a href="mailto:support-escalation@mergify.com">support-escalation@mergify.com</a>

Use the following template to help our on-call engineer start investigating
immediately:

```text
Subject: [ESCALATION] <your-org> — <brief description>

Repository: <org/repo>
Pull Request: <link to the affected PR>
Observed behavior: <what is happening>
Expected behavior: <what should happen>
Started at: <approximate time + timezone>
Recent config changes: <any changes made before the issue>
```

:::tip
  Include as much context as possible. Logs, screenshots, and links to CI runs
  help us triage faster and reduce back-and-forth.
:::

## What Happens Next

1. **Acknowledgment** — within 1 hour, an engineer confirms receipt and begins
   triage.

2. **Investigation** — we analyze the incident using the details you provided
   alongside our own telemetry.

3. **Resolution** — a fix or workaround is deployed. We confirm the outcome with
   you before closing the incident.

Resolution time varies depending on incident complexity.

## What's Covered

This service covers critical incidents where Mergify's core features are
unavailable or actively blocking your workflow:

- **Merge Queue** is unavailable or non-responsive.
- **Workflow Automation** is failing to trigger or execute.
- **Merge Protections** are blocking merges unexpectedly.

## What's Not Covered

- Issues caused by third-party systems (GitHub outages, your CI/CD
  infrastructure).

- Configuration questions or product guidance.

- Feature requests or non-urgent assistance.

- Problems unrelated to Mergify's core services.

:::note
  For these topics, reach out through the [standard support channels](/support).
:::

## Your Part

To help us resolve your incident as fast as possible:

- **Use the template above** — include repository, PR, and observed behavior.

- **Stay reachable** — ensure a technical contact is available for follow-up
  during triage.

- **Reserve escalation for blocking events** — this channel is for genuine
  service-blocking incidents that you cannot resolve using the product's
  available features.
