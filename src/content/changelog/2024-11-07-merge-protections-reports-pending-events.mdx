---
title: Merge Protections reports pending events
date: 2024-11-07
tags:
  - Merge Protections
description: "Merge protections rules that includes pending conditions (such as CI jobs running or schedule) will now report the status of merge protections to *pending*."
---

Merge protections rules that includes pending conditions (such as CI jobs running or schedule) will now report the status of merge protections to *pending*. This makes sure that merge protections respects the expected outcome of the conditions written.
