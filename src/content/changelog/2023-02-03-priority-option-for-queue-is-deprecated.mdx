date: 2023-02-03
tags:

---

The `priority` option for the queue action is now deprecated and will be removed.

They should be replaced by the new [`priority_rules`](https://docs.mergify.com/actions/queue/#priority-rules)[ sections](https://docs.mergify.com/actions/queue/#priority-rules).
