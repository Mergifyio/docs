description: "Test your regex patterns against sample log lines directly in the auto-retry rule builder."
---

You can now test your log patterns before saving them. When configuring auto-retry rules, a new "Test your log patterns" section lets you paste a sample log line and instantly see whether it matches any of your defined regex patterns. This helps you validate patterns before they go live, reducing trial-and-error.
