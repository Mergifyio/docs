---
title: "Trace a restarted batch back to its previous attempt"
date: 2026-07-08
tags:
  - Merge Queue
description: "The merge queue activity log now links a restarted batch to the previous batch it retried."
---

When a batch restarts because its previous attempt was aborted, the merge queue activity log now shows a link to that earlier batch. Clicking it opens the previous batch — including its checks and why it stopped — even after it has left the queue, so you can trace a restart end to end instead of hitting a dead end.
