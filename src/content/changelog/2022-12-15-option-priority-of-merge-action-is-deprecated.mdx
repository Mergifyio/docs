title: Option priority of merge action is deprecated
date: 2022-12-15
tags:
  - Deprecations
description: "The `priority` option of the merge action has been marked as deprecated and will be removed on January 17, 2023."
---

The `priority` option of the merge action has been marked as deprecated and will be removed on January 17, 2023. This option was not documented and has no impact on Mergify behaviour.

There is no replacement for this option.
