---
title: "Slack notifications for quarantined tests"
date: 2026-06-19
tags:
  - Test Insights
description: "Mergify can now post a Slack message whenever a test is quarantined or dequarantined, so your team sees mitigation activity in real time."
---

Subscribe any Slack channel to test quarantine activity from **Integrations → Slack**. Mergify posts a message whenever a test is quarantined or dequarantined — by a teammate or automatically — with a link to the test, who made the change, the test's current health status, and the reason it was quarantined. Choose the repositories and events each channel follows.
