The Self-hosted Runners page now answers "is my fleet correctly sized?" at a glance. A capacity view shows fleet utilization with a 100% saturation line, plus active runners against your fleet ceiling. A new Queue tab charts how long jobs wait to acquire a runner over time, grouped by the label-set they request — filter by labels and read the per-label-set breakdown to spot which runner pools are starved and size your fleet accordingly. Configuration moved to its own page so the metrics stay front and centre.
