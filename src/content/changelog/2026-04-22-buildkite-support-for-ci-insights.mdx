title: "Buildkite support for CI Insights"
date: 2026-04-22
tags:
  - CI Insights
description: "Connect your Buildkite pipelines to Mergify CI Insights to track build and job performance alongside your GitHub Actions and Jenkins data."
---

Mergify CI Insights now supports Buildkite as a CI provider. Configure a Buildkite organization webhook pointing to Mergify and paste your CI application key as the webhook token — build and job events will automatically appear in your CI Insights dashboards alongside GitHub Actions and Jenkins.
