date: 2023-12-21
tags:

description: "Mergify now exposes the `queue-frozen` and `queue-freeze-reason` attribute to be used in conditions and templates."
---

Mergify now exposes the `queue-frozen` and `queue-freeze-reason` attribute to be used in conditions and templates. This allows to know if a pull request is in a queue that is frozen and the reason for the freeze.
